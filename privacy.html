# Marin-chan Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** July 15, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how Marin-chan handles information. ## Information We May Store Depending on the enabled features, Marin-chan may store: * Discord User IDs * Discord Server IDs * Channel IDs * Role IDs * Command settings * Ticket data * Leveling and experience data * Moderation records * Giveaway information Marin-chan does **not** collect passwords, payment information, or private Discord messages. ## Purpose Stored information is used only to provide the bot's features and improve functionality. ## Data Sharing We do not sell or share user data with third parties, except where required by law or by Discord's services. ## Data Removal Server administrators may request removal of server-specific data by contacting the Manime Lounge staff, where technically possible. ## Changes This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. ## Contact For privacy-related questions, please contact the Manime Lounge staff through the official Discord server.
